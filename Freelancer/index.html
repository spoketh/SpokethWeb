Questions Our Clients Ask Us

It's alright to ask us questions. It's our priority to make sure you know what you're getting.

When can I message you? When can I expect a reply back?

You can send us a message at any time during the day, and expect a response or a proposal back within 48 hours. If we cannot finish your project by the time you need it, we will let you know as immediately as possible.

Can I meet with you in person to talk about my project?

Of course! In fact, that's our preferred method of doing business with our clients. Just send us an email with your phone number to to set up a meeting. Unfortunately, we currently cannot meet with clients outside of Anchorage, AK.

When/How will I pay you?

After a proposal is agreed upon, we will require at least half of the agreed price in our proposal to be paid before we start working on your project. We accept payments online through Paypal. In person, we accept payments through cash or credit card.

Why are your services so cheap compared to everyone else?

We base our pricing on the ability of small businesses to afford our work. Both of us have lived in Anchorage for the majority of our lives, and we'd hate to see our local businesses lose out to national companies that have bigger budgets for similar services.

I can't afford your services. Can I still have you do work for me?

We might be able to, depending on our schedule. Just send us a message with the budget that you have for your project. We'll let you know what we will be able to do for you.

Why does it seem that newer websites all look the same?

It's what you don't see that really matters- websites now have to be built for phones, tablets, and desktops, and many companies are realizing that having one website that works well on every single device is more effective than having multiple disjointed versions. We think that it's important as well. What's changing is that more and more customers are looking up businesses with their phones- what hasn't changed is the importance of making the best first impression.
